About Us

Trusted Technology Partner in Ethiopia

Founded in 2023, Etacom Technology has quickly established itself as a reliable provider of software, AI, data, data center, and ICT solutions. With our headquarters in Mekelle and operations across Ethiopia, we serve hotels, enterprises, NGOs, government agencies, and manufacturing firms.

We combine deep technical expertise with a passion for innovation to deliver solutions that drive efficiency, unlock insights, and create lasting value for every client we serve.
